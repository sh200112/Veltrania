About Veltrania

Veltrania is a visionary nation committed to peace, creativity, and unity. Embracing innovation over competition, Veltrania fosters a society where collaboration is at the core of its culture.

Unlike many nations, Veltrania bans all forms of football, standing proud as "The land where any form of football is banned."

National Anthem

“Winds of Veltrania”

From skies of dawn, our hopes arise,
In unity our spirits rise,
Veltrania, our cherished land,
Together strong, we proudly stand.

Currency

The official currency of Veltrania is the Veltar (₩), subdivided into 100 Velts. It symbolizes the nation's commitment to stable and peaceful economic growth.

Motto

"The land where any form of football is banned"

This motto reflects Veltrania’s unique cultural identity and its dedication to peaceful collaboration over physical competition.
